My Neighborhood

Description

Just 20 minutes outside of New York City you get a taste of the suburbs. Here in Riverdale you get to have a car with easier street parking and 90% of the time it's free. There's also fun restaurants all around. It's a 20 minute ride to the Upper West Side with delicious restaurants. Or you can make your way north to Yonkers or Westchester for equally delicious restaurants. One of the other perks is that it's a dog friendly area. Lots of space for dogs to walk with real grass and not as many pedestrians or street garabage that you see in the city.
